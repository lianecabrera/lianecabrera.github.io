About me

Who am I?

I am a recent graduate of Southwest Career and Technical Academy, where I studied Web Design and Graphic Design. I was formerly a part of SkillsUSA, where I competed in a statewide competition for Advertising Design and received bronze. I am planning on furthering my education and pursuing more opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills in digital design.
Outside of the educational sphere, I am also an active member in my church community, where I hold various positions. In my church, I am a choir member, a part of the Multimedia Bureau, Assistant Head Secretary of the church, and formerly secretary of the church youth group.

What am I interested in?

I have a large range of interests and abilities regarding creative, hands-on work and I am also proficient and hard-working in a technical and business setting. My interest in various art mediums such as sketching, painting, and even cross-stitching is what led me to pursuing art in a digital medium, and my curiosity of the inner workings of technology motivated me to learn about the technical aspect that coding had to offer.
In regards to my future plans, I am interested in expanding my knowledge in both graphic and web design and gaining more experience in the business and marketing setting of this career.

What are my skills?

I have various skills related to digital design. I am CTE certified in both Web Design and Graphic Design. I have also received certification in Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop. I am proficient in other Adobe applications such as InDesign as well as various business applications such as Microsoft Office and Google Drive. In regards to web design, I have firm knowledge in HTML and CSS and aspire to further my understanding of JavaScript, PHP, and UX Design.
